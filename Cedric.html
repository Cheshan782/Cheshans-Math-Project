Cedric

Cedric is a struggling inventor with a wonderful tech invention that will make lives easier , however, this invention is still in the sketch phase and will be for a while. He is single with no kids and needs a calm work environment, he is 21 years old. He doesn't want an extraordinary lifestyle, as long as his inventions reach many hands. He has a part-time job to make ends meet and to allow him to find time to make his invention. Therefore he is making $2648. It is not a lot so he needs to start sharing stuff.

Housing

We will get him a studio apartment in Calgary and make him share with a roommate. The average rent for a studio in Calgary is $1569 Zumper. We will find 1 roommate who will also be his business partner. It may seem hard to share a studio apartment, but it is doable spacewise. Now his rent is $784.5, this is good for him to rent because permanent living situations are only good for people with stable incomes, however, Cedric does not have that so its better to get a mad landlord than a bank.

Account Options

The account I recommend him having is a TFSA account because of tax-free you can take it out when you get much richer but you won't be holding that as your income. Although the limit is $6,500 I would recommend keeping the leftover and keeping it liquid as it is needed to build prototypes and to invest into that invention of yours. A TFSA account is technically liquid because you can take it out whenever you want, however, you're going to get taxed for it as the government recognizes it as income. Stocks are good investments in your situation but Cedric should only invest 20% of his savings after every other expense is paid.

Transportation

Lastly, his car. I wouldn't recommend he get a car, because I don't think it would be worth it in Calgary because there is nowhere to go, therefore I would tell him to invest in a bus pass. I would go on the bus 20 times a month which is 10 trips. I think that is plenty for someone whose only reason for going out should just be for food or getting materials for his invention. An adult fare is $3.60 and lasts 90 minutes fares. Therefore monthly he should spend somewhere around $72.

For other things, he should spend $300 including food, clothes, tech, and wifi cable. This leaves $1491.5 he should do 80% ($1196.8) investing in himself and 20% ($298.3) savings for the future. I think Cedric should take this risk because he is already deeply invested in his invention, so this budget will set him up for success, with a little emergency money.
